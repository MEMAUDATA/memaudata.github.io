Nicolas Vannson

Ph.D. in Neurosciences.

I’m a former audiologist who transitioned into data science to solve one problem: turning clinical complexity into actionable intelligence. After more than a decade working with hearing‑impaired patients and leading quantitative research, I founded Hearing Data Science in 2025 to help audiology organizations elevate patient care through advanced analytics, machine learning, and AI‑driven decision support
