Nicolas Vannson

Ph.D. in Neurosciences.

Hi! I'm a former audiologist turned data scientist. From audiology to data science in January 2024, I've applied a decade of experience in data analysis, statistics, and teaching to explore the power of data. In September 2025, I founded Hearing Data Science, a company dedicated to advancing audiology through clinical data analysis and AI.
